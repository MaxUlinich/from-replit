# from-replit
from replit

## backgammon:
Press on a piece to move it according to the left side dice value shown in the middle of the screen. Press on the dice values to swap them (left vs right).

## musicalfalling:
Bouncy balls spawn where you click. They play sounds when they fall on the colored tiles.

## octopus simulation:
Use buttons to spawn octopi and predators to simulate how adaptations and mutations help in nature. Press space to pause the simulation and click on an octopus while paused to get more info. 

## rock-paper-scissors:
Game where rocks, papers, and scissors bounce around the screen and convert each other. You can control the one in the red box using arrow keys. Press Space to randomize.

## wordle:
Version of wordle using a custom word list. The shorter list of words is the options that the secret word could be. The longer list of words is the acceptable guesses. Does not appear to be working at the moment due to python updates.
